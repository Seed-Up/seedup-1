TikTalk

Project, Voice Synthesis

TikTalk brings text to life. Our text-to-speech API uses the latest speech synthesis and deep learning technologies, allowing mobile users to switch from text to audio just pressing a button. You are reading an article on your favourite newspaper webapp in the underground, then this is your stop : do you really want to stop reading? Do you really want to read while walking ? Just press the button and listen to the end of the article with a natural and pleasant voice. We will automatically pick up the reading where you left it. Newspapers are one thing, but imagine the same thing for your emails or other things.

Made by Edouard Malet, Paul Poupet,

Ckdo

CDiscount, App

A birthday to celebrate? A leaving do to organise? A wedding to attend? Money pots are perfect for any occasion. Online money pots are even more useful. But after raising the money, you still have to find the gift or go and buy it. That is why we developped a website for Cdiscount, using Cdiscount API to allow you to create a money pot for a specific occasion, you just have to invite your friends (using sms, email, facebook automatic invitations), and give some ideas of gifts. All the participants will be allowed to vote or to add new ideas. Then our algorithm matches the ammount raised with the money pot, the different ideas proposed by the participants and the different votes to order the best gift for the people you want to offer the gift.

BeTheSound

Project, Audio

We created a free solution that allows you to synchronize all your audio devices: phones, computers and every speakers -even the oldest- either by plugging directly a phone to the speaker (jack-to-jack) or using a harware module that we wre ccurrently creating. We broadcast sound via using a state of the art technologies (webRTC, webAudioAPI, NTP protocols…) to recreate a sound system, a home-cinema only with existing equipments. It is SONOS, but for free. -> 1st prize of the Startup week-end of Ecole Centrale Paris -> Jury's prize for the startup week-end of Ecole Polytechnique -> 1st prize of the innovation competition : "Be the future" -> "Shape the future" prize at the competition 'Petit Poucet', BeTheSound is mentionned in the 10 best projects of the year.

Made by Paul Poupet, Benjamin Poilvé Robin Lambertz
